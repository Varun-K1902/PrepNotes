Welcome to PrepNotes

PrepNotes is your ultimate destination for BTech study materials! Our mission is to provide students with notes for various subjects, making exam preparation easier and more efficient. Whether you're cramming for finals or looking for resources to help you understand topics, PrepNotes is here to help you succeed.

Our Story

The idea for PrepNotes was born out of my own experience as a BTech student. During exam times, I often found myself struggling to find proper notes to study. I had to scour different websites, presentations, and textbooks just to find the answers I needed. This led me to compile my own set of notes for various subjects, gathering information from multiple sources. By the end of my BTech, I had comprehensive notes for almost every subject. I realized that other students could benefit from my notes, and that’s how PrepNotes came to life!

How It Works

Using PrepNotes is easy! Simply navigate to the subject of your choice, browse through the list of questions, and find the answers you need. Our notes are organized by subject, making it simple to locate exactly what you’re looking for. Whether you're preparing for exams or just want to deepen your understanding of a topic, PrepNotes is here to guide you.
